MINDS Wireframe

Sample Video to showcase a prototype on how the MINDS app will work and its basic functions to help for intellectual disabled childrens.

Thought Process

Since it was an App for Intellectual disabled children I decide to make the UI easier to see, the buttons bigger and easier to press. I also made the design very children friendly having a space theme so that it will look entertaining for them to use
