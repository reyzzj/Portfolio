Short Description

Exercise tracker was make to let the user enter their own username to proceed on to choose their level 1,2 and 3, the user then have to clear level 1 then can proceed to level 2 and subsequently. The highest level is level 3. The 3rd page will require the user to enter the duration that he/she exercise, it will then be calculated if the user meet the requirement which is more than 10 hours a week to move on to level 2.

Thought process

The colors are made very light and clear so that it will be soothing to the eye and, without too much animation to make the website feels extremely overwhelmed and have a cool vibes at the same time.
