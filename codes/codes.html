This was an event poster that I created for class work.


Design writeup

I decided to choose this sketch to be my design as I felt that it has more potential in presenting an attractive, bright, and appealing product advertisement. In my final artwork, I applied Contrast, Emphasis and Balance as the Principles of Design.

For Contrast, I decided to play with the size and make my middle doughnut as the spotlight. By doing so, I increase the size for the middle doughnut and make the others smaller. For Emphasis, I decided to play use Proximity. I clutter some doughnuts at the edge and on in the middle to show that the main target object is the doughnut. For Balance, I use asymmetrical balance as the elements/objects are all not mirror opposite.
